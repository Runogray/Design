Meet MYA

Shop the best skin care products from all your favourite brands at mya. Choose from body lotions, facial serums, oils & more! Buying made easy with fast payments included.

Product Expectation

At mya, get perfect product recommendations tailored to your unique skin type and desired ingredients. Easily save a product to purchase later in your favourites, create wishlist's and share with a special someone. The app provides a very seamless and effective tracking service for users to monitor their purchases from the shop to their destinations.

Every user experience has been specially thought through and research conducted to make life so much easier for new and returning customers.

Return and Refund Policy

Products can be returned and a refund or exchange will be made if the necessary requirements are met. The customer requesting for a return would be expected to fill out the return form accessible in the MYA support option. Customers would receive a respond as soon as a return ticket is generated within the app.

Perks

  • Refund flexibility
  • Get email notification upon response to request
  • Add images to description on return request
  • Return multiple damaged items
  • Request for order image before packaging

Tracking

Track your deliveries, monitor shipments as they transit from our store to your destination and get 24/7 access to customer service for any issues or questions. We have an excellent return policy so you can fill out the form at convenience when necessary and receive a fast response!

Perks

  • Track items within Lagos and outside Lagos within the app
  • Get email notification as delivery progresses
  • Worried about delivery delay can be resolved by a quick in app chart or speak customer service agent for assistance
  • Personal information are safe
  • Secured order completion code for accepting delivery
  • Door step delivery option

Product Objective

Increasing Sales: The primary objective of this app is to drive sales and increase revenue. The app is designed to encourage customers to make purchases by showcasing the most effective skin care products, offering discounts and promotions, and providing easy payment options.

Enhancing Customer Experience: MYA is designed to provide customers with a seamless and enjoyable shopping experience. This can be achieved by providing features like easy navigation, filtering options, product recommendations, and personalized shopping suggestions.

Overall, the objective of an e-commerce skincare app is to provide a user-friendly, convenient, and enjoyable shopping experience for customers, while driving sales and building brand loyalty.

Design Process

Empathise

Understanding the product users and what they need and their expectations about buying beauty products online.

Define

Analyse user expectations and create a user persona to highlight users most frustrating encounters using an e-commerce application.

Ideate

Brainstorming the defined solutions in a the best possible implimentation method. Information architecture, sketching and wireframing.

Prototype

Low fidelity designs and high fidelity designs to visualize the product expectations. Final prototyping for team collaboration and structuring page layout.

Test

Usability testing and feedback with the developers and stockholders.

The first step for an e-commerce skincare app was to make an examination and evaluation of the competing apps in the same market. The goal of this analysis is to identify the strengths and weaknesses of each competitor, as well as opportunities and threats they pose to the success of the e-commerce skincare app.
