Magenta Casher

Magenta is a company with the intend to solve stressful payment and track business performance, by making a lifestyle application.

Magenta is built strictly for the purpose of satisfying the daily struggle of moving around with your debit card, manually checking business performance, and the frustration of waiting for a debit or credit alert from your local bank.

User Expectation

Casher can access branch dashboard after admin generate a unique branch Id and password. This unique Id and password can be used by a permitted device to access the casher dashboard.

Features

  • As a merchant accept payments even from cards from all over the world that would normally not be accepted
  • Track payment history, statistics and download invoice
  • Generate payment Id and scan code for customers
  • Accept payment and print transaction receipts

Magenta Admin

The admin dashboard is designed to keep track of every sales activity from the casher dashboard, create multiple business branches and monitor them from a single dashboard on any device.

User Expectation

After a user has successfully created a business account on magenta pass, business owners can choose to add multiple branches to business profile.

Features

  • Get instant update on your sales across branches of your business
  • Track payment history, statistics and download invoice
  • Add multiple branches to business profile
  • Make withdrawal at ease

Magenta User

Make payment easily by scanning a payment code or receiving a payment link on your mobile device. Accessibility to sharp sharp

User Expectation

Users can download the magentapass app from app store or google play store and maximize the flexibility of online payments. Also, new users who do not want to download the app can scan the generated payment code or get a payment link for quick payment.

Features

  • Help keep your card details to yourself, and have only one point of entry to all your cards
  • Receive payment link for quick transaction
  • Scan and make payment off the app
  • Download receipts and report payment issues
  • 24/7 customer service

Strategy Used

  • made sure to put together every design layouts for different pages and screens, color style, typography, buttons, gradients, inspirations, design style and padding structure.
  • Used collaboration tools for quick reach outs to get task done faster.
  • I worked with a structured design system that can easily be understood and accessible for the developers.
  • Standups helped us keep track of progress and made sure everyone on the team was productive.
  • Worked with a senior UX designer to carry out product research and market analytic for possible business setbacks and product engagement.
  • high fidelity responsive design and prototyping came next.

Unique Features

Ease of payments, less delay, expense history and organization, access to payment in an issuer or formfactor agnostic fashion

User Research

  • During this research we understood the advantage of making payment by scanning a qr code from the merchant screen by reducing multiple steps of payment completion.
  • From a business owner view of the need to generate a payment receipt using any device available.
  • A good amount of users would rather experience the application without downloading it and a few would prefer to download the application then experience it.
  • Out of 10 business owners interviewed about the flexibility of not using a pos machine to make payment transaction rather optimizing an app that works as both a register and payment platform. Only two still preferred the bogus method they are used to.

Competitive Analysis

The main aim for a competitive analysis was to research major competitors to gain insight into their products, sales, and marketing tactics. Implementing stronger business strategies, satisfying a larger scale of user needs, and standing out with a unique feature.
