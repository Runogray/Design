Design Process

Empathise

Understanding the clients and what they expect using a real estate web application and ways to make their experience much easier.

Define

Analyse all product users expectations and create a user persona to highlight users most frustrating encounters using the home web application.

Ideate

Brainstorming the defined solutions in a the best possible implantation method. Information architecture, sketching and wireframing.

Prototype

Low fidelity designs and high fidelity designs to visualize the product expectations. Final prototyping for team collaboration and structuring page layout.

Test

Usability testing and feedback with the developers and stockholders.

Competitive analysis

Market research carried out on 3 popular real estate planforms was analysed during the product research process for better product development and accessibility for users.

Research

I took some of the most critical reviews on the trustpilot and tripadvisor as a basis for improvement on user experience.
